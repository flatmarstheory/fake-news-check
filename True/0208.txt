OffbeatEdited by Bhavya SukhejaUpdated: March 31, 2023 7:23 pm ISTThe images were created using an AI application called Midjourney.Images generated by Artificial Intelligence (AI) have been making waves recently, and now one artist has used this technology to show how 'Game Of Thrones' characters would look like they were dressed by an "Indian costume designer". Taking to Instagram, AI enthusiast Jyo John Mulloor posted a series of images showing GoT characters - including Daenerys Targaryen, Jon Snow, Arya Stark and many others - in royal Indian attire. "If George R. R. Martin has hired an Indian costume designer for Game of Thrones," the caption of the post read. Take a look below: View this post on InstagramA post shared by Jyo John Mulloor (@jyo_john_mulloor)In the caption, Mr Mulloor revealed that he created the pictures using an AI application called Midjourney. The first picture of the post showed the character Tyrion Lannister wearing red and yellow attire. The next picture showed Daenerys Targaryen donning yellow and blue attire. The rest images showed several other characters from the HBO series, including Cersei Lannister and Bran Stark.  Mr Mulloor shared the images just a few days back, and since then the post has accumulated more than 18,000 likes.
