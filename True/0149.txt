Samsung Galaxy M13 Summary
Samsung Galaxy M13 mobile was launched on 14th July 2022. The phone comes with a 6.60-inch touchscreen display (FHD+). Samsung Galaxy M13 is powered by
an octa-core Samsung Exynos 850 processor. It comes with 4GB, 6GB of RAM. The Samsung Galaxy M13 runs Android 12 and
is powered by a 6000mAh battery.
The Samsung Galaxy M13 supports proprietary fast charging. As far as the cameras are concerned, the Samsung Galaxy M13 on the rear packs a triple camera setup featuring a 50-megapixel (f/1.8) primary
camera;
a 5-megapixel (f/2.2, ultra wide-angle)
camera, and
a 2-megapixel (f/2.4)
camera. The rear camera setup has autofocus. It has a single front camera setup for selfies, featuring
an 8-megapixel sensor with an f/2.2 aperture.The Samsung Galaxy M13 runs One UI 4 is based on Android 12 and packs 64GB, 128GB of inbuilt storage that can be expanded via microSD card (up to 1024GB). The Samsung Galaxy M13 measures 165.40 x 76.90 x 9.30mm (height x width x thickness)
and weighs 207.00 grams. It was launched in Aqua Green, Midnight Blue, and
Stardust Brown colours. Connectivity options on the Samsung Galaxy M13 include Wi-Fi
802.11 a/b/g/n/ac, GPS, Bluetooth v5.00, and
USB Type-C. Sensors on the
phone include
accelerometer,
ambient light sensor,
compass/ magnetometer,
proximity sensor, and
fingerprint sensor.As of 1st April 2023, Samsung Galaxy M13 price in India starts at Rs. 9,699.
