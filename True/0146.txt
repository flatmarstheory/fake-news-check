Realme C55 Summary
Realme C55 mobile was launched on 7th March 2023. The phone comes with a 90 Hz refresh rate 6.72-inch touchscreen display (FHD+). Realme C55 is powered by
an octa-core MediaTek Helio G88 processor. It comes with 4GB, 6GB, 8GB of RAM. The Realme C55 runs Android 13 and
is powered by a 5000mAh battery.
The Realme C55 supports Super VOOC fast charging. As far as the cameras are concerned, the Realme C55 on the rear packs a dual camera setup featuring a 64-megapixel primary
camera, and
a 2-megapixel
camera. It has a single front camera setup for selfies, featuring
an 8-megapixel sensor.The Realme C55 runs Realme UI 4.0 is based on Android 13 and packs 64GB, 128GB of inbuilt storage. It was launched in Sunshower and
Black colours. Connectivity options on the Realme C55 include Wi-Fi,
GPS, and
USB Type-C.As of 1st April 2023, Realme C55 price in India starts at Rs. 10,999.
