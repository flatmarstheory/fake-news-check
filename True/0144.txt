OnePlus Nord CE 2 Lite 5G Summary
OnePlus Nord CE 2 Lite 5G mobile was launched on 28th April 2022. The phone comes with a 120 Hz refresh rate 6.59-inch touchscreen display offering a resolution of
1080x2412 pixels (FHD+) at a pixel density of 402 pixels per inch (ppi) and an aspect ratio of 20:9. OnePlus Nord CE 2 Lite 5G is powered by
an octa-core Qualcomm Snapdragon 695 processor. It comes with 6GB, 8GB of RAM. The OnePlus Nord CE 2 Lite 5G runs Android 12 and
is powered by a 5000mAh battery.
The OnePlus Nord CE 2 Lite 5G supports Super VOOC fast charging. As far as the cameras are concerned, the OnePlus Nord CE 2 Lite 5G on the rear packs a triple camera setup featuring a 64-megapixel (f/1.7, 0.7-micron) primary
camera;
a 2-megapixel (f/2.4)
camera, and
a 2-megapixel (f/2.4)
camera. The rear camera setup has autofocus. It has a single front camera setup for selfies, featuring
a 16-megapixel sensor with an f/2.0 aperture and a pixel size of 1.0-micron.The OnePlus Nord CE 2 Lite 5G runs OxygenOS 12.1 is based on Android 12 and packs 128GB of inbuilt storage that can be expanded via microSD card. The OnePlus Nord CE 2 Lite 5G is a dual-SIM (GSM and GSM) mobile that accepts Nano-SIM and Nano-SIM cards. The OnePlus Nord CE 2 Lite 5G measures 164.30 x 75.60 x 8.50mm (height x width x thickness)
and weighs 195.00 grams. It was launched in Black Dusk and
Blue Tide colours. Connectivity options on the OnePlus Nord CE 2 Lite 5G include Wi-Fi
802.11 a/b/g/n/ac, GPS, Bluetooth v5.20, USB Type-C, 3G, 4G (with support for Band 40 used by some LTE networks in India) , and
5G. Sensors on the
phone include
accelerometer,
ambient light sensor,
compass/ magnetometer,
gyroscope,
proximity sensor, and
in-display fingerprint sensor.As of 1st April 2023, OnePlus Nord CE 2 Lite 5G price in India starts at Rs. 18,999.
