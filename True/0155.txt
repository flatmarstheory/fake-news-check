Lenovo ThinkPad E460 Summary
Lenovo ThinkPad E460 is a DOS laptop with a 14.00-inch display that has a resolution of 1366x768 pixels. It is powered by a Core i5 processor and it comes with 4GB of RAM. The Lenovo ThinkPad E460 packs 1TB of HDD storage. Graphics are powered by AMD Radeon R7 M360. Connectivity options include Bluetooth, Ethernet and it comes with 3 USB ports (3 x USB 3.0),
HDMI Port, Multi Card Slot ports.As of 1st April 2023, Lenovo ThinkPad E460 price in India starts at Rs. 46,800.
