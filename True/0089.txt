Ahead of the start of the tournament, here are 5 oldest players in the history of the tournament. ZoomChris Gayle: Recently inducted into Royal Challengers Bangalore's 'Hall of Fame', Chris Gayle is fifth in the list. At 42 years and seven days old, Gayle played for Punjab Kings against the Mumbai Indians during IPL 2021. ( BCCI ) facebook twitter whatsapp reddit ZoomImran Tahir: Set to don the commentators hat during IPL 2023, Imran Tahir is fourth on the list. At 42 years and 29 days old, Tahir was part of Chennai Super Kings' squad during their title winning season in 2021. However, he did not play a single game. ( Twitter/CSK ) facebook twitter whatsapp reddit ZoomMuttiah Muralitharan: Currently a part of SunRisers Hyderabad's coaching staff, former Sri Lankan spin wizard Muttiah Muralitharan is third on the list. At 42 years and 35 days old, Muralitharan played his last IPL game for RCB in 2019. ( BCCI ) facebook twitter whatsapp reddit ZoomPravin Tambe: After making his debut for Rajasthan Royals in 2013, Pravin Tambe became the oldest debutant in the history of the tournament. At 44 years and 219 days, Tambe is second on the list, having played his last game in 2016 for the now defunct Gujarat Lions. ( AFP ) facebook twitter whatsapp reddit ZoomBrad Hogg: At 45 years and 92 days old, former Australia spinner Brad Hogg tops the list. Hogg played his last IPL game against Gujarat Lions in May 2016 for KKR.
