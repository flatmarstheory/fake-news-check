From Priyanka Chopra To Deepika Padukone: Stars Descend Upon NMACC Launch Event
Many stars such as Priyanka Chopra, Hansika Motwani, Kareena Kapoor and Deepika Padukone arrived at the Nita Mukesh Ambani Cultural Centre event in Mumbai.
Bollywood News
| Written By
Nitish Vashishtha
1/11
Image: Varinder Chawla
Varun Dhawan posed with his Bhediya co-star Kriti Sanon at the NMACC event in Mumbai today. While Varun looked sharp in a suit, Sanon wore a monochrome dress.
2/11
Image: Varinder Chawla
Actor Hansika Motwani looked elegant in a peach-pink saree. She wore sparkly jewellery to compliment her outfit.
3/11
Image: Varinder Chawla
Sania Mirza wore an eye-catching silver, glittery outfit.
4/11
Image: Varinder Chawla
Priyanka Chopra appeared at the event in a silver sheer gown with ruffled sleeves.
5/11
Image: Varinder Chawla
Priyanka Chopra also posed with her husband Nick Jonas, who wore an all-black suit.
6/11
Image: Varinder Chawla
Kareena Kapoor posed with her sister Karisma Kapoor.
7/11
Image: Varinder Chawla
Shraddha Kapoor looked elegant in an all-white gown.
8/11
Image: Varinder Chawla
Sidharth Malhotra and Kiara Advani looked like a match made in heaven at the NMACC event.
9/11
Image: Varinder Chawla
