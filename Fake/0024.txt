MAJOR GAURAV ARYA
Major Gaurav Arya (Retd) (born 25 September 1972) is the Consulting Editor for Strategic Affairs at Republic Media Network. He analyses the Indian Defence services, National Security and Strategy. A former Indian Army Major, he served in the Military from 1993 to 1999 and was deployed with the Kumaon Regiment. He is a public speaker and talks about defense, leadership, motivation, team building, strategic affairs, national security and capability building. Major Gaurav Arya is often seen on prime-time TV debates and has also spoken at various TED Talks (TEDx). Major Gaurav Arya has has a very large following on social media platforms and often shapes opinion on various topics of discussion concerned with National Security.
