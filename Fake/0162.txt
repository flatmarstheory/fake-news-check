Grievance Redressal
Grievance Cell
Republic Network Channels, are a member of the News Broadcasters Federation. Our aim is, in all cases, to resolve complaints about the content on our Channels as satisfactorily and quickly as reasonably possible.
Any complaint relating to content shall be made by the person aggrieved within a reasonable time not exceeding 14 (Fourteen) days from the date of first broadcast. Your complaint should include the following:
the name/title of the broadcast you are complaining about
the date and time of the broadcast and the channel on which it was broadcast
the nature of the complaint, giving reasons
your name and contact details (anonymous complaints will not normally be considered)
