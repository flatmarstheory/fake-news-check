Beta testers can now edit photos, videos, and GIFs in the text editor, reports WABetaInfo.
Users will also be able to easily switch between different fonts by tapping one of the font options displayed above the keyboard. (Also Read: Man Loses Apple Watch In Sea, Finds Days After In Working Condition - Here's How)
