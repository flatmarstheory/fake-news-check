Sachin Tendulkar, Yuvraj Singh, Jasprit Bumrah: Cricketers Snapped At NMACC Event
Cricketers such as Yuvraj Singh and Sachin Tendulkar and more arrived at the NMACC event in Mumbai. Many stars also attended the NMACC event.

Image: Varinder Chawla
Cricket legend Sachin Tendulkar arrived at the NMACC event with his wife Anjali Tendulkar and daughter Sara Tendulkar.

Image: Varinder Chawla
Sara Tendulkar stole the limelight as she wore an all-black dress with sparkly jewellery.
