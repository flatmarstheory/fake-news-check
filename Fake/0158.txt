Republic (Republic TV and Republic World) is India's first independent media tech company.
Republic TV
Republic TV is India's most-watched English news channel since its week of launch. Republic TV was launched on 6 May 2017. The channel was launched as a free-to-air channel through DTH services and cable television operators across the country. Republic TV has nationalism at its core, the pursuit of truth as its guiding principle and imbibes the fearlessness to investigate, dig out and broadcast that truth in order to fix accountability for a billion. Being home to the youngest and most insatiable news team in India, Republic has broken the norms of traditional newsrooms and journalistic setups.
Republic TV’s Editorial Committee is composed of Editorial Members Abhishek Kapoor, Rhythm Anand Bharadwaj, Shawan Sen and Niranjan Narayanaswamy.
The Editorial Committee wholly helms the editorial management of the channel including news flow, production of news, ensures news authenticity and provides the editorial strategy for Republic TV. It is entirely responsible for the content generated on the channel, and the integrity of the same. The Editorial Committee was formed as per due process and is certified by the Company Secretary of ARG Outlier Network Mr. Mohit Dhamne.
As the digital wing of Republic TV, RepublicWorld.com is a platform built for the next generation of news. Seeped in hard-hitting opinion and wound with interactive news content and formats, Republic World follows the principle of breaking the news and breaking the silence.
Republic Bharat
Republic Bharat, Republic Media Network’s Hindi news channel, was launched in February 2019 and has stormed the genre with its breakthrough formats and razor focus on hard news, investigations, accountability seeking and impact-driven journalism-- principles which have ensured that in a short span, Republic Bharat has soared to become a leading Hindi news channel across India.
Republic Bharat’s Editorial Committee is composed of Editorial Members Abhishek Kapoor, Rhythm Anand Bharadwaj, Aishwarya Kapoor and Vimal Kumar Singh.
The Editorial Committee wholly helms the editorial management of the channel including news flow, production of news, ensures news authenticity and provides the editorial strategy for Republic Bharat. It is entirely responsible for the content generated on the channel, and the integrity of the same. The Editorial Committee was formed as per due process and is certified by the Company Secretary of ARG Outlier Network Mr. Mohit Dhamne.
Republic Bangla
Republic Bangla is the Bangla news endeavour of Republic Media Network. Republic Bangla aims to bring in a new era of journalism in West Bengal that is fearless, unhindered by agendas and uncompromising on the truth. Armed with the tagline “Kotha hobey chokhe chokh rekhe” and Republic Bangla has become a name to reckon with in the Bengali news genre.
Republic Bangla’s Editorial Committee is composed of Editorial Members Subhrangshu Chatterjee and Mayukh Ghosh.
The Editorial Committee wholly helms the editorial management of the channel including news flow, the production of news, ensures news authenticity and provides the editorial strategy for Republic Bangla. It is entirely responsible for the content generated on the channel, and the integrity of the same. The Editorial Committee was formed as per due process and is certified by the Company Secretary of ARG Outlier Network Mr. Mohit Dhamne.
